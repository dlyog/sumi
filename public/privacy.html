Privacy and educational use

1StopQuantum is an AI experiment and learning demo powered by Sumi, an AI Learning Companion. This build was created for the OpenAI Hackathon and is not a general-public service.

No sensitive records

Do not enter personal, payment-card (PCI), health (PHI), or other sensitive information. This local educational app is not designed to connect those data types.

Sumi and generated content can make mistakes. Results are educational simulations, not professional, medical, financial, legal, or production advice. There is no guarantee or warranty, and the service may be changed, interrupted, or discontinued at any time.

Local-first voice

When enabled, speech recognition and speech playback use the configured local development services. Review your deployment configuration before exposing the app to other users.
