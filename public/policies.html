Privacy

Accounts, learning activity, feedback, and administrator settings are stored in the PostgreSQL database configured by the operator. The application does not implement sale of personal information or cross-context behavioral advertising. Do not enter sensitive personal data. Contact the operator of your installation for access, correction, or deletion requests.

Transparency

Sumi uses local MLX Whisper for speech recognition, an administrator-selected OpenAI-compatible language model, and local Kokoro for generated speech. Prepared lesson media may also be AI-assisted. Simulations run locally and are not results from physical quantum hardware.
